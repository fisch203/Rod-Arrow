Rod and Arrow is a group of hunting and fishing enthusiasts from Mid-Michigan.

We are interested in providing a convenient place for fellow enthusiasts
to view educational and entertaining videos ranging from GoPro field footage
to instructional videos on cooking and cleaning your catch. We will share videos
from YouTube and other video sites that we find interesting and/or informative.



Soon, we will begin releasing original video content including footage from
Daniel's bear hunting trip to Alaska, Matt's Muskie trips on Lake St Claire
and Alex in the kitchen preparing what they bring back.
